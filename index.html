Welcome to NTUAIS Lab

Welcome to the National Taiwan University Artificial Intelligence Security Lab. Our research centers on trustworthy machine learning, focusing on robustness, privacy, and fairness . To bridge the current trustworthiness gap in ML, it is essential to address these interconnected challenges within a cohesive framework. This involves developing solutions that consider their complex interdependencies, aiming to create reliable, secure, and equitable machine learning systems, thereby fostering greater public trust in the technology.
